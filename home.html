Project Overview

This project focuses on developing AI-ML based models to predict prices of agri-horticultural commodities such as pulses and vegetables (onion, potato, etc.). Our models leverage historical data and advanced machine learning techniques to provide accurate price forecasts.

Key Features

Real-Time Predictions: Get up-to-date price forecasts based on current market data.
Customizable Forecasting: Choose your forecasting needs (daily, monthly, quarterly).
User-Friendly Interface: Designed for ease of use by farmers, traders, and policymakers.

How It Works

Our models utilize various machine learning algorithms, including Random Forest and Neural Networks, to analyze historical pricing data, weather conditions, and market trends. This data-driven approach enhances the accuracy of price predictions.

Benefits of AI-ML in Agriculture

AI and ML can lead to better pricing strategies, improved yield management, and enhanced market access. By predicting prices accurately, stakeholders can make informed decisions that benefit their operations.
